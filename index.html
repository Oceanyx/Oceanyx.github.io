About

Naturally curious.

Ever since I was a teenager, I've been drawn to the structures around me — not just how they function, but why they’re organized the way they are. Why do we choose one structure over another? Is it for convenience? Efficiency? Development? Or is it the result of habit, oversight, or some deeper pattern we’ve yet to recognize?

That curiosity naturally led me to code — a space where abstract ideas take shape and systems are built from the ground up. As a full-stack developer, I’m drawn to both the architecture and the details: how different layers of technology interact, and how thoughtful design choices ripple through a system. Whether it's building APIs, refining user interfaces, or optimizing infrastructure, I find purpose in making things that are not only functional, but deeply coherent.
