About Hi, I'm Brian

Some inspiring words to describe yourself

Curious, driven, and endlessly passionate about growth — that’s how I’d describe myself. I believe in showing up with heart and intention in everything I do. Whether I’m solving a complex problem, creating something meaningful, or simply learning something new, I bring a grounded sense of purpose and a quiet determination to keep moving forward. I’m not just chasing goals — I’m building a life aligned with values, creativity, and connection.

How I Got Here

Short version: a lot of lessons, a few wrong turns, and one persistent gut feeling that I’m meant to build something that matters. Long version? I’ve followed the breadcrumbs of curiosity through challenges, doubts, and moments that stretched me way outside my comfort zone — and honestly, I’m better for all of it. The path wasn’t straight, but it was mine.

My Values & Beliefs

Be Human First

Before titles, before to-do lists — we’re all people. I believe in kindness, directness, and not taking yourself too seriously. While our world revolves around systems, individual humans are the ones in it, not the abstract concept, "people."

Stay Curious, Stay Humble

I don’t pretend to know it all. I’d rather ask good questions and keep learning than act like I’ve got it all figured out. Often, when we don’t understand something — or someone else’s perspective — it is most likely because our scope is too small.

Perspective Shifting

Each person's viewpoint is like a unique vector, with its own magnitude, direction, and origin. Our position in the conversation shapes how we contribute, whether forcefully or subtly. Magic happens when we shift our views and realize that even the wildest deviations can lead to breakthroughs. After all, what’s life without a little displacement from the norm?

Fail, Learn, Repeat

Growth usually comes dressed as chaos. I try, I learn, I try again — ideally with fewer forehead slaps each time. I find patterns and strategize — whether that means zooming in to catch the details or zooming out to see the bigger picture — whatever it takes to find the right resolution for the task.

Why Work With Me

I don’t just check boxes — I bring ideas to life. I’m someone who listens deeply, communicates clearly, and gets things done without losing sight of the bigger picture. I believe in real collaboration, mutual respect, and pushing past the expected. If you’re looking for someone who brings both heart and hustle, I’m your person. I bring more than just skills — I bring presence, perspective, and a genuine commitment to delivering value in a way that feels human, not transactional.

A Few More Words About Myself

Outside of work, I’m just trying to be a better human — reading things that make me think, chasing quiet moments, and occasionally winning fake arguments in the shower. I believe in depth over noise, in laughing through the mess, and in doing work that actually reflects who I am. While I primarily value truth first and foremost in all its perspectives, I've come to have a deep appreciation in the need to deliver it in a way that resonates with an audience. I believe that having both is necessary not just to have a successful career, but to become a better human in general who can control the flow of the narrative.
